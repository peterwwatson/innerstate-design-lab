Explainer design · round 1 · for feedback

Four ways to explain the same practice. Which one works?

This is one screen of a dark, meditative breathing app — the "here's what it is and why" page. Below are four competing designs of that exact screen: same content, four different approaches (a journey-map spine, a story, a two-column split, and a picture-per-screen). They're design candidates, not the finished product.

Skim all four top to bottom, or use the numbered buttons below to jump straight to one. Each preview scrolls on its own inside its frame; a "back to top" link sits at the end of every section. When you've looked, the three questions at the very bottom are what I'd love answered.
